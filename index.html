à la levant

exploring levantine food, culture, and community-building in Berlin

The Levant, the region centered around modern Syria, Lebanon, Israel, Palestine, and Jordan, is one full of culture, tradition, and migration. How does food from this area embody, transcend, and build community?
We took the melting pot city of Berlin as a case study and developed à la levant, a delicious collection of interviews and photos reflecting Levantine expression through food.
